The Present

We use the present when we talk about things happening now, or things that happen regularly. In Gudjal, we use the -ya ending to talk about the present. It is similar to the -s or -ing verb endings in English.

Gabaridhanda-ya.
rainfall-PRES
Rain is falling.
Gariyinda-yaguwa.
sunset-PRESwest
The sun sets in the west.

The -ing and -s endings mean slightly different things in English. Notice the difference between "he runs" and "he is running". In Gudjal, the -ya ending can be used for both meanings.
