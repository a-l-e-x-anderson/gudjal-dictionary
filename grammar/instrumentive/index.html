The Instrumentive Case

In Gudjal, we use the noun ending -nggu to say that we used that noun to do something. If I said "I hit him with a stick", "with a stick" would have an instrumentive meaning.

Banggala-nggumuga-nangaya.
spear-INSTRget-PASTI
I got it using the spear.
Marndila-nggumuga-ya!
hand-INSTRget-IMP
Grab it! Get it with your hands!
